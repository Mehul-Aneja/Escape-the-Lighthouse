Whatever lies in the coffin patiently waits as you slowly approach it.

It has a rusty faded-black look with what appears to be a layer of dirt over it. This combination of dirt and a fading color makes it hard to clearly observe it against the dim lighting. It almost feels as if the coffin was camouflaging itself.
Was it trying to hide from you? No, of course not... Coffins can't hide...

You spot no visible markings or inscriptions on the coffin. Puzzled and creeped out, you make up your mind about your next move.
