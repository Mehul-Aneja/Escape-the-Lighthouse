As the newly appointed keeper, you had just arrived at the Sanguis Lighthouse ...



Your head hurts a lot. Struggling to keep your balance, you glance around the tiny, windowless, bare room.
As your eyes gain focus, you see a sight of concern—dried blood splattered over the wall spelling in poor cursive—

"YOUR STRENGTH COMES FROM BRAVERY AND CAUTION, KINDNESS AND CARING!
YOUR WEAKNESS COMES FROM THE TASTE OF YOUR BLOOD..."

A rush of emotions overwhelm you and you stumble, slamming into the only other object in the room: a wooden door. The door gives under your weight and flies off its hinges, banging on the floor with a loud
THUD
As you peer through, a poorly lit staircase spiralling downward signals your only way out.
