LEVEL II


The darkness slowly fades as a dim light emanates from the entrance of the room. An inscription above the room reads "LEVEL II"

Bravely yet cautiously, you peer into the room. Two lanterns hang along the circular walls, bathing the room in soft, flickering yellow light. A much needed change from what you've experienced so far. From the floor above, the slimy substance drips down to the floor and slips through the cracks on the floorboard. With help from the light emanating from the two lanterns, you might be able to get a better look at the small puddle.
