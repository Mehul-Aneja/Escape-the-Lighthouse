You step toward the odd little creature, aiming to pierce its little rat heart with your stake.


With all your might, you thrust the stake down but the rat is too nimble and dodges to the side.

ARGHH!

You let out a scream as the rat pounces at you. You feel a rush of pain enveloping you as it bites into your neck. Stumbling back, you quickly grab it and right as you swing it at the wall, you step onto the puddle and lose your footing.

SQUEAK

It squeaks in pain and scutters away into the darkness. You take a moment to rest and inspect your wounds. There is a small bite mark on your neck, nothing else. You seem and feel healthy.

You decide to move on as you nurse the fresh bite on your neck.
