You approach the puddle of fluid, careful not to step on it.

Immediately, you notice a strange iron-rich odor emanating from it. Moreover, it appears too viscious to be a simple oil leak. It also seems to be colorless and looks as if it is absorbing a good amount of light falling on its surface. While wondering what this strange fluid could be, your eye catches a sharp movement near the corner of your vision.

SQUEAK

The rat dashes toward you. Almost as if practicing politeness, it halts a few feet away and balances itself on its hind legs. Knocking its head to one side, the rat gazes at you expectantly.

You have a hard time digesting the situation, but your mind is (almost) as sharp as a stake.
